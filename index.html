Prototype tools designed around structured inputs, transparent outputs, reusable workflows and human review.

Recruiter AI Screening Assistant

A browser prototype that compares a candidate CV against a role brief, scores the match against must-have and nice-to-have criteria, highlights evidence from the CV, flags missing or weak areas, and suggests interview questions for human review.

Recruitment Review CV Screening Candidate Comparison

What it demonstrates

  • Structured CV-to-role matching logic
  • Evidence-based screening outputs instead of vague AI judgement
  • Candidate saving, comparison and export workflows

Business value

Helps make early-stage recruitment screening more consistent, auditable and easier to compare, while keeping the final decision with a human reviewer.

Stack
HTML, CSS, JavaScript, local scoring rules

AI Job Application Assistant

A browser prototype that analyses a job description against a candidate profile, highlights missing keywords and generates tailored CV, cover letter, outreach and interview-prep drafts.

Job Search Fit Analysis Application Drafts

What it demonstrates

  • Structured input design for job and candidate evidence
  • Weighted scoring for skills, experience and project relevance
  • Recruiter-style first impression and risk review outputs

Business value

Turns a stressful application process into a repeatable review workflow with clearer next actions.

Writing Assistant Trainer

A browser prototype that helps turn writing samples, style rules, prompt templates and feedback notes into a structured writing assistant setup that can be reused across job applications, LinkedIn posts, README files and interview preparation.

Writing System Prompt Design Personal Style Training

What it demonstrates

  • Prompt system design for tone, structure and reusable writing rules
  • Safe handling of writing samples and private examples
  • Export workflow for building a personal writing assistant

Business value

Makes personal writing support more consistent, reusable and easier to improve over time, especially when producing repeated professional materials.
